Design principles

The logic that keeps the platform readable, scalable, and reusable.

One runtime, many scenarios

Keep the engine stable and move content into packs.

Cloud by default

Heavy rendering and compute live in the cloud so clients stay thin.

Clear separation of concerns

Simulation, inference, quantum, and task distribution are independently replaceable.

Human‑in‑the‑loop research

Players are part of the scientific process, especially where intuition matters.

Portable across projects

Stable modules become reusable assets: pack format, UI, task brokers, and workflows.

Open science model
